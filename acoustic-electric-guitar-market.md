<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Pickup</li><li>Piezo Pickup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic-electric guitar market features two primary pickup types: electromagnetic and piezo. Electromagnetic pickups utilize magnets and coils to detect string vibrations, producing a rich, warm tone suitable for blues and rock genres. Conversely, piezo pickups, placed under the bridge, capture vibrations directly from the guitar body, offering a more natural sound ideal for folk and acoustic styles. Both systems enhance playability and versatility by enabling electric amplification, catering to diverse musical preferences and performance settings.</p></p>
