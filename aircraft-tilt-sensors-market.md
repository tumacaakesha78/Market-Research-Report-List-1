<p><p>The aircraft tilt sensors market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital sensors. Analog tilt sensors measure angle changes through continuous voltage or current outputs, providing real-time data useful for immediate adjustments in aircraft orientation. In contrast, digital tilt sensors convert the angle measurements into digital signals, allowing for more precise data processing and integration with advanced avionics systems. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing safety and performance in aviation by monitoring aircraft stability and orientation.</p></p>
