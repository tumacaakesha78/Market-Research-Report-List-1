<p><p>The API Pipe market comprises three primary types: Hot Rolled, Cold Rolled, and Cold Drawn pipes. Hot Rolled pipes are produced at high temperatures, resulting in a rough surface and lower costs, ideal for structural applications. Cold Rolled pipes undergo processing at room temperature, offering tighter tolerances and improved surface finishes, suitable for precision applications. Cold Drawn pipes are further processed to enhance strength and dimensional accuracy, making them ideal for high-pressure applications and specialized industries.</p></p>
