<p><p>The Automatic License Plate Reader (ALPR) market is categorized into two main types: fixed type and portable type. Fixed type ALPR systems are installed in stationary locations, such as roadsides or entrances, providing continuous monitoring and data collection for traffic management and law enforcement. In contrast, portable type ALPR systems are mobile and can be easily deployed on vehicles or temporary setups, offering flexibility for various enforcement operations. Both types enhance vehicle identification, aiding crime prevention and traffic control.</p></p>
