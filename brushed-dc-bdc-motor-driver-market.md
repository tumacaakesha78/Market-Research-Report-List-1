<p><p>The Brushed DC (BDC) Motor Driver market consists of three primary types: Voltage Drive, Chopper Drive, and PWM Drive. Voltage Drive directly controls motor speed by varying the input voltage. Chopper Drive modulates voltage in high-frequency pulses, improving efficiency by reducing heat. PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) Drive regulates the motor's average power by adjusting the duty cycle of the pulse, enabling precise speed control while minimizing energy loss. These methods cater to diverse applications and performance requirements in industrial and consumer markets.</p></p>
