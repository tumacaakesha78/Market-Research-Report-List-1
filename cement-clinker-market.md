<p><p>Cement clinker is a crucial intermediate product in the production of cement, composed primarily of calcium silicates, aluminates, and ferrites. It is produced by heating limestone and clay in a kiln at high temperatures, which results in hard nodules that are ground into powder to make cement. The quality and composition of cement clinker directly influence the properties of the final cement product, making it essential for construction applications.</p><p>The Cement Clinker Market is witnessing steady growth due to increasing urbanization, infrastructure development, and rising demand for housing globally.
