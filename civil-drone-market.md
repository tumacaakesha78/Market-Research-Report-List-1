<p><p>The civil drone market comprises two primary types: fixed-wing drones and rotary-wing drones. Fixed-wing drones resemble traditional aircraft, featuring long flight times and covering extensive distances, making them ideal for applications like agriculture, surveying, and mapping. In contrast, rotary-wing drones, including quadcopters, offer vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, excelling in tasks requiring agility and maneuverability, such as aerial photography, inspection, and delivery services. Both types play vital roles across various sectors, driven by advancements in technology and expanding use cases.</p></p>
