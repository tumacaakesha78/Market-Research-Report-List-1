<p><ul><li>Movable</li><li>Unmovable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clean room booths are categorized into two main types based on their mobility: movable and unmovable. Movable clean room booths are designed for flexibility, allowing easy relocation to accommodate different projects or changes in workflow. They are ideal for dynamic environments that require adaptability. On the other hand, unmovable clean room booths are fixed installations, providing a stable and controlled environment for processes that demand strict adherence to cleanliness standards. These booths are suited for long-term applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals and biotech.</p></p>
