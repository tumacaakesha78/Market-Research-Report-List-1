<p><p>The colored contact lenses market is segmented into three main types: soft contact lenses, hybrid contact lenses, and rigid contact lenses. Soft contact lenses are made from flexible materials, allowing for comfort and ease of use. Hybrid contact lenses combine a rigid gas-permeable center with a soft outer ring, offering the benefits of both types. Rigid contact lenses, made from durable materials, provide clear vision and are suitable for specific vision corrections. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
