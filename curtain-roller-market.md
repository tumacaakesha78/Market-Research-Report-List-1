<p><p>The curtain roller market is primarily divided into two types: electromechanical and manual curtain rollers. Electromotion curtain rollers utilize electric motors for effortless operation, allowing users to open or close curtains with a switch or remote control, enhancing convenience and efficiency. In contrast, manual curtain rollers rely on traditional mechanisms such as pull cords or rods, requiring physical effort but often featuring a simpler design and lower cost. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and needs, balancing automation with hands-on control.</p></p>
