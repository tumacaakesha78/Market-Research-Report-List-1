<p><p>The data cable market is divided into three main types: twisted pair cables, coaxial cables, and optic fibers. Twisted pair cables, typically made of copper, are widely used for networking due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. Coaxial cables, also copper-based, offer superior bandwidth and are often used for cable television and internet services. Optic cables, made from fiber, provide high-speed data transmission over long distances with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and high-performance networks.</p></p>
