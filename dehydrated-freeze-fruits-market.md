<p><p>Dehydrated freeze fruits are fruits that have undergone a process of freeze-drying, where they are initially frozen and then subjected to low pressure, allowing ice to evaporate without passing through a liquid state. The convenience of lightweight and long-lasting products makes dehydrated freeze fruits popular for snacking, baking, and ingredient applications in various food products.</p><p>The dehydrated freeze fruits market is witnessing significant growth, primarily driven by rising consumer demand for healthy snacks and convenient food options.
