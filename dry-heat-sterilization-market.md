<p><p>The dry heat sterilization market is primarily categorized into flame sterilization and hot air sterilization. Flame sterilization utilizes an open flame to quickly eliminate microorganisms on instruments, making it ideal for immediate use in labs and clinical settings. Hot air sterilization, on the other hand, employs controlled high temperatures in an oven to ensure thorough sterilization of heat-resistant equipment and materials over a longer period, suitable for items that cannot withstand moisture. Both methods are crucial for effective infection control.</p></p>
