<p><strong>The Electrical Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Main Breaker</li><li>Main Lugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical panels market is primarily categorized into two types: Main Breaker panels and Main Lugs panels. Main Breaker panels feature an integrated circuit breaker that provides overcurrent protection and disconnect capability. These are typically used in residential and commercial applications, offering enhanced safety. In contrast, Main Lugs panels lack a main breaker, relying on external disconnects, making them suitable for subpanel applications where a main breaker is unnecessary. Both types are essential for managing electrical distribution and ensuring safety.</p></p>
