<p><p>Expandable microspheres are tiny, spherical particles made of a thermoplastic shell encapsulating a gas. When heated, the gas expands, causing the shell to soften and expand, increasing the volume of the microspheres. These materials are widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, healthcare, and personal care due to their lightweight, insulating, and space-filling properties. Applications include lightweight fillers, coatings, adhesives, and sealants.</p><p>The Expandable Microspheres Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period.
