<p><strong>The Flyash Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class F</li><li>Class C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fly ash is categorized into two primary market types: Class F and Class C. Class F fly ash, typically produced from burning anthracite or bituminous coal, has low calcium content and is primarily used in concrete production for its pozzolanic properties. Class C fly ash, derived from lignite or sub-bituminous coal, contains higher calcium levels, making it self-cementing and suitable for structural applications. Both types enhance concrete durability, reduce environmental impact, and improve workability in construction projects.</p></p>
