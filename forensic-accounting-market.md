<p><p>Forensic accounting encompasses various market types, including criminal and fraud investigation, bankruptcy proceedings, and risk management. In criminal and fraud investigations, forensic accountants analyze financial data to detect illegal activities and support legal actions. In bankruptcy proceedings, they assess financial health, uncover asset concealment, and aid in the equitable distribution of resources. In risk management, these accountants evaluate financial risks, advise on controls, and help organizations mitigate potential fraud or financial misconduct, ensuring greater fiscal integrity and compliance.</p></p>
