<p><ul><li>Passive Evaporation (Air-Drying) Systems</li><li>Vacuum Drying Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gel dryer market encompasses various drying technologies, primarily including passive evaporation (air-drying) systems and vacuum drying systems. Passive evaporation systems use ambient air to facilitate moisture removal from gels, relying on natural airflow and temperature variations. In contrast, vacuum drying systems create a low-pressure environment, enhancing evaporation rates and allowing for gentler drying of heat-sensitive materials. Both systems cater to diverse applications, offering advantages in efficiency, controlled drying conditions, and maintenance of product integrity in various industries.</p></p>
