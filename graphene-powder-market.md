<p><strong>The Graphene Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer Flakes</li><li>Multilayer Flakes</li><li>Nanoplatelets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphene powder market includes various types based on structural layers and forms. **Monolayer flakes** consist of single-atom-thick graphene sheets, offering exceptional electrical, thermal, and mechanical properties, ideal for advanced electronics and composites. **Multilayer flakes** contain a few stacked graphene layers, balancing performance and cost, suitable for energy storage and coatings. **Nanoplatelets** are small, stacked graphene fragments with high surface area, used in polymers, lubricants, and conductive inks. Each type caters to specific applications, driving innovation across industries.</p></p>
