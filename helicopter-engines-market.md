<p><p>The helicopter engines market comprises three main types: piston engines, gas turbine engines, and stamping engines. Piston engines, typically found in smaller helicopters, use reciprocating motion to generate thrust and are cost-effective for light aircraft. Gas turbine engines, prevalent in larger helicopters, offer greater power and efficiency, making them ideal for military and commercial applications. Stamping engines, while less common, utilize a unique mechanism that converts pressure into rotational motion, catering to specific niche markets within helicopter propulsion.</p></p>
