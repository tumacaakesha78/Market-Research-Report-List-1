<p><p>The holographic grating market comprises primarily two types: plane type and concave type holographic gratings. Plane type holographic gratings feature a flat surface, dispersing light in a uniform manner, ideal for applications requiring high diffraction efficiency. In contrast, concave type holographic gratings possess a curved surface, allowing for compact designs and enhanced light collection capabilities, making them suitable for spectrometers and imaging systems. Both types serve critical roles in optical devices, influencing their performance and application versatility.</p></p>
