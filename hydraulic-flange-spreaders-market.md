<p><p>The hydraulic flange spreaders market comprises two main types: split type and integral type. Split type flange spreaders feature two separate hydraulic cylinders that allow for adjustable spreading across various flange sizes, ideal for complex applications. Integral type spreaders, on the other hand, are designed as a single unit with all components built into one body, providing a compact and user-friendly solution for straightforward tasks. Both types are essential for safe and efficient flange maintenance in various industries.</p></p>
