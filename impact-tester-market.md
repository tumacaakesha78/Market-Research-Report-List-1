<p><p>The impact tester market encompasses various testing methods to evaluate the toughness of materials. The Charpy test measures the energy absorbed during fracture by striking a notched specimen with a pendulum, providing important data on impact resistance. The Izod test, similar in principle, involves securing the specimen vertically and striking it at a notch, yielding insights into material behavior under stress. Other testing methods may include drop weight or dynamic tests, offering additional evaluation techniques for specific application requirements.</p></p>
