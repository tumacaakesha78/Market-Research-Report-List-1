<p><p>The lamination adhesive market consists of three primary types: solvent-based, solventless, and water-based adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives utilize organic solvents for better bonding and flexibility but may pose environmental concerns. Solventless adhesives offer a low-impact option with high performance, lacking volatile compounds, making them safe for various applications. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly, using water as a carrier, which reduces harmful emissions. Each type serves specific industries, including packaging, automotive, and construction, based on performance and safety requirements.</p></p>
