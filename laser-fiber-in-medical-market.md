<p><p>In the medical market, laser fibers are categorized into two main types: single-use and reusable medical laser fibers. Single-use medical laser fibers are designed for one-time use, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination and ensuring optimal performance during procedures. They are typically employed in settings requiring high hygiene standards. Both types cater to various medical applications, enhancing precision and patient outcomes.</p></p>
