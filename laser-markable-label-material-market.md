<p><p>The laser markable label material market is segmented into antistatic and non-antistatic types. Antistatic materials are designed to dissipate static electricity, making them ideal for use in electronic environments where static discharge could damage sensitive components. Non-antistatic materials, on the other hand, do not offer this property and are often used in general applications where static is not a concern. Each type caters to specific needs, ensuring effective labeling in various industries and conditions.</p></p>
