<p><p>The Liquid Alum market is segmented into two primary types based on iron content: Iron Content and Free of Iron. Iron Content Liquid Alum contains iron impurities, making it suitable for applications where iron presence is acceptable, such as wastewater treatment and industrial processes. Free of Iron Liquid Alum is purified, removing iron, making it ideal for sensitive applications like drinking water treatment, paper manufacturing, and cosmetics, where iron contamination could compromise quality or safety.
