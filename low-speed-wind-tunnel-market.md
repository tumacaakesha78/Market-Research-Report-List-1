<p><strong>The Low Speed Wind Tunnel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-speed wind tunnel market is categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal wind tunnels. Vertical wind tunnels direct airflow upwards, commonly used for testing small-scale models and simulating scenarios like free-fall conditions. In contrast, horizontal wind tunnels allow airflow to travel parallel to the ground, ideal for aerodynamic testing of larger models, such as vehicles and aircraft. Both types are crucial for research and development in aerospace, automotive, and engineering industries, enabling detailed aerodynamic analysis and design optimization.</p></p>
