<p><p>The Metallic Label Market comprises two main types: Cold Foil Metallic Labels and Hot Foil Metallic Labels. Cold foil labels utilize a cold foil application process, where adhesive is printed and then metallic foil is applied, creating vivid metallic effects without heat. In contrast, hot foil labels involve applying heat and pressure to transfer a metallic foil onto the label substrate, resulting in a durable and striking finish. Both methods enhance product appeal, making them popular in various industries, particularly in packaging and branding.</p></p>
