<p><p>The microwave absorber material market is categorized into low permeability and high permeability types. Low permeability materials typically exhibit minimal magnetic loss, making them suitable for applications requiring less absorption, such as radar systems. In contrast, high permeability materials have greater magnetic loss properties, allowing them to absorb microwaves more efficiently. These are ideal for high-frequency applications where effective energy dissipation is critical, such as in telecommunications and electronic devices, enhancing overall performance and signal clarity.</p></p>
