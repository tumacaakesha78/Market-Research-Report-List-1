<p><strong>The Motor Graders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Frame Motor Grader</li><li>Articulated Frame Motor Grader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motor graders market is primarily categorized into two types: rigid frame and articulated frame motor graders. Rigid frame motor graders feature a fixed chassis design, providing stability and precision for grading tasks, ideal for applications requiring consistent depth control. In contrast, articulated frame motor graders have a pivot joint between the front and rear axles, enhancing maneuverability and allowing for tighter turns, making them suitable for uneven terrains and confined spaces. Both types are essential for construction and road maintenance.</p></p>
