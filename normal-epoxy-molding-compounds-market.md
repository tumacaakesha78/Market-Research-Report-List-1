<p><ul><li>Crystalline Silica Powder Type</li><li>Fused Silica Powder Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Normal epoxy molding compounds are classified into three main types based on the filler material used. Crystalline silica powder type employs crystalline silica for enhanced mechanical properties and thermal resistance. Fused silica powder type uses fused silica, offering superior thermal stability and low thermal expansion, making it suitable for high-performance applications. The "Other" category includes various alternative fillers and additives that enhance specific properties, such as improved flow, lower viscosity, or resistance to chemicals, catering to diverse molding requirements in various industries.</p></p>
