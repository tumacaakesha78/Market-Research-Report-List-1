<p><ul><li>Web Offset Printing Machines</li><li>Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offset printing machines market is primarily divided into two types: web offset printing machines and sheet-fed offset printing machines. Web offset printing utilizes continuous rolls of paper, making it ideal for high-volume production like newspapers and magazines, enabling faster printing speeds. In contrast, sheet-fed offset printing machines handle individual sheets of paper, offering flexibility for shorter runs and a variety of formats. This type is often used for quality printing products like brochures, business cards, and packaging.</p></p>
