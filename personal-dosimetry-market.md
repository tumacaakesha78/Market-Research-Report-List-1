<p><p>The personal dosimetry market consists of various types of dosimeters used to measure radiation exposure. Thermoluminescent dosimeters (TLD) utilize heat to release stored energy as light, indicating exposure. Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL) dosimeters use light stimulation to measure radiation. Radium Phosphate Luminescent (RPL) dosimeters are sensitive to low doses and provide real-time monitoring. Active dosimeters feature electronic components that provide immediate readings and continuous monitoring, enhancing safety in radiation-prone environments. Together, they ensure comprehensive radiation safety management.</p></p>
