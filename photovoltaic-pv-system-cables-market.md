<p><p>The Photovoltaic (PV) System Cables market is categorized primarily into DC cables and AC cables. DC cables are used to connect solar panels to inverters, transmitting direct current generated by the panels. They are designed to handle high voltage and resistance to environmental factors. In contrast, AC cables carry the converted alternating current from the inverter to the grid or electrical systems. Both types are essential for ensuring efficiency, safety, and longevity in solar energy applications, contributing to the overall performance of PV systems.</p></p>
