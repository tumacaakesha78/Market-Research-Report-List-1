<p><p>The Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) fibers market is primarily categorized into PPS filaments and PPS staple fibers. PPS filaments are continuous strands used in applications requiring high strength and thermal resistance, ideal for industrial textiles and specialized fabrics. In contrast, PPS staple fibers are short fibers utilized in various non-woven products and insulation materials, offering excellent chemical and thermal properties. Both types are valued for their durability and performance in demanding environments, making them essential in automotive, aerospace, and electronics industries.</p></p>
