<p><strong>The Portable Spray Tan Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP)</li><li>High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable spray tan machine market is characterized by two primary types: Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP) and High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP). LVLP machines use less airflow and are ideal for achieving a fine mist, resulting in a more controlled application, making them suitable for home users and smaller settings. In contrast, HVLP machines produce a higher volume of spray with lower pressure, allowing for faster application over larger areas. These machines are commonly preferred in professional salons for efficiency and speed.</p></p>
