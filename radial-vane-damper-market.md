<p><p>The radial vane damper market comprises three main types: manual, pneumatic, and electric. Manual types operate through physical adjustment, allowing users to control airflow manually. Pneumatic types utilize compressed air to adjust vanes, providing automatic response to pressure changes, making them suitable for industrial applications. Electric types employ motors to automate vane movement, offering precise control and integration with smart systems. Each type serves specific needs in ventilation and air management across various sectors, enhancing system efficiency and performance.</p></p>
