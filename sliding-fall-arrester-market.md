<p><p>Sliding fall arresters are crucial safety devices in work environments at height. The market is divided into two main types: non-retractable and retractable. Non-retractable fall arresters typically have a fixed length of cable or rope, allowing for a straightforward, cost-effective solution, though they may require manual adjustments. Conversely, retractable fall arresters feature an automatic retraction mechanism, providing enhanced mobility and ease of use. This allows workers to move freely while ensuring immediate locking during a fall, adding an extra layer of safety.</p></p>
