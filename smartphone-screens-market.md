<p><p>The smartphone screens market features several main types: LCD, OLED, AMOLED, and others. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) offers good color accuracy and is cost-effective but lacks deep blacks. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) delivers vibrant colors and contrast, allowing for thinner designs. AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) enhances OLED by improving response times and power efficiency, making it popular in premium devices. Other screens may include Mini-LED and MicroLED technologies, offering advanced features for enhanced display quality.</p></p>
