<p><p>Sodium PCA is available in two market forms: L-form and DL-form. The L-form is the natural, biologically active type, primarily used in skincare and cosmetic products for its moisturizing and anti-aging properties. In contrast, the DL-form is a synthetic version that combines both L- and D-forms, often utilized for its stability and cost-effectiveness in various applications, including food and pharmaceuticals. Understanding these distinctions helps manufacturers choose the appropriate form for their specific industry needs.</p></p>
