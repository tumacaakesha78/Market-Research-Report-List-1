<p><p>Two-phase immersion cooling is a method utilized to dissipate heat from electronic components by submerging them in a dielectric fluid. This innovative technology relies on the principles of phase change, where the fluid absorbs heat, evaporates, and then condenses back, efficiently transferring heat away from critical components without the need for traditional air cooling systems or additional cooling apparatus. </p><p>The Two-phase Immersion Cooling Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for efficient cooling solutions in data centers, high-performance computing (HPC), and other electronic applications.
