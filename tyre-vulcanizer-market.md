<p><ul><li>Mechanical Vulcanizer</li><li>Hydraulic Vulcanizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tyre vulcanizer market includes two main types: mechanical and hydraulic vulcanizers. Mechanical vulcanizers utilize mechanical components to apply heat and pressure, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations and quick repairs. In contrast, hydraulic vulcanizers use hydraulic pressure for more uniform heat distribution and are ideal for larger production environments, offering enhanced precision and efficiency. Both types cater to the growing demand for tyre maintenance and manufacturing, driven by the expanding automotive industry and increasing vehicle usage worldwide.</p></p>
