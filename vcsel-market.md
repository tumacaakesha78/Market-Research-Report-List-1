<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VCSEL (Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Laser) market is categorized into single mode and multimode segments. Single mode VCSELs offer a narrow emission wavelength, resulting in high precision and longer transmission distances, making them ideal for applications like long-haul fiber optic communication. Multimode VCSELs, with a broader emission area, support multiple light paths, facilitating high-speed data transfer over shorter distances, making them suitable for data centers and short-range networking. Each type caters to specific performance needs in various optical communication applications.</p></p>
