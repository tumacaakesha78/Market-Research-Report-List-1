<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC gas meter market is primarily divided into two types: PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID meters utilize UV light to ionize gas molecules, offering high sensitivity and rapid response for detecting volatile organic compounds. In contrast, MOS sensors rely on the change in electrical conductivity of a material when exposed to gases, providing a cost-effective solution for various applications. Both technologies serve to monitor air quality and ensure safety in diverse environments.</p></p>
