<p><strong>The Wind Power Converter System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Doubly-Fed</li><li>Full Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind power converter system market comprises two primary types: Doubly-Fed Induction Generators (DFIG) and Full Power converters. DFIG systems utilize a partially rated converter, allowing them to connect the rotor circuit to the grid. This configuration offers better efficiency and cost-effectiveness, particularly for variable wind conditions. In contrast, Full Power converters use a fully rated system to connect both the rotor and stator to the grid, providing enhanced grid compatibility and control, making them ideal for larger wind farms and more stringent grid requirements.</p></p>
