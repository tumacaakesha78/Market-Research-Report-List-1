<p><p>The XLPE insulated high voltage cable market features two primary types: single-core and three-core cables. Single-core XLPE cables are designed for applications requiring a straightforward, efficient installation with less insulation material, typically used in overhead lines or underground installations. Three-core XLPE cables, on the other hand, consist of three conductors bundled together for balanced load distribution, making them ideal for compact installations in urban environments and improving thermal performance. Both types provide superior electrical properties and resistance to environmental factors.</p></p>
